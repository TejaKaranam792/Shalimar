About Hotel Shalimar

Hotel Shalimar is a highly-rated, popular casual dining restaurant located near Municipal Park on Begusarai Road in Vizianagaram (Cantonment), Andhra Pradesh. Known for our exceptional value for money and unforgettable taste, we specialize in authentic Hyderabadi Biryani, flavorful North Indian curries, and tantalizing Chinese cuisine.

Whether you're craving our highly recommended Chicken Dum Biryani, Mutton Fry Biryani, Prawns Biryani, or our sizzling Tandoori specialties, every dish is crafted with passion and perfection. We offer comfortable dine-in experiences, convenient takeaways, and fast delivery through Swiggy and Zomato.
