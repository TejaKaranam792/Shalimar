A Legacy of Authentic Hyderabadi Cuisine

Hotel Shalimar Restaurant is renowned for bringing the royal flavors of Hyderabad to Vizianagaram. Popular among both locals and tourists, we pride ourselves on delivering an unforgettable culinary experience.

Our hallmark is the traditional slow-cooked Dum method. Each dish is carefully crafted with the perfect blend of spices, tender meat, and aromatic basmati rice, ensuring every bite is a celebration of flavor.
